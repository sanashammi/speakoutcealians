UGC Anti-Ragging Regulations (2009)

  • Ragging is a punishable offense under the University Grants Commission (UGC) Regulations.
  • Expulsion, suspension, and legal action for offenders.
  • Imprisonment (up to 3 years) and fine (₹50,000) under IPC.

Indian Penal Code (IPC) Sections

  • Section 323 & 325: Punishment for causing hurt.
  • Section 506: Criminal intimidation (threatening someone).
  • Section 509: Outraging the modesty of a woman (gender-based harassment).
